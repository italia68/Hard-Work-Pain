Our story

PAIN IS
PROGRESS

Hard Work & Pain was built by someone who knows what it's like to drag themselves to the gym after a long day, to push through the last rep when every muscle is screaming to stop — and to come back the next day and do it all over again.

This isn't a platform for weekend warriors or fitness influencers. It's a space for people who are serious about the work. People who track their lifts, respect the process, and understand that real results take real time.

We built Platemate because every other tracking app felt like it was designed for someone else. Too complicated, too flashy, or too basic. Platemate is simple, fast, and built around how serious lifters actually train.

Hard Work & Pain is the community, the content, and the mindset behind the app. Welcome.
