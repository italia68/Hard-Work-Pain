Step Zero

Install on Any Device

Platemate is a Progressive Web App (PWA). That means it works like a native app — no download needed — and you can add it to your home screen for instant access.

🍎 iPhone & iPad — Safari
1
Open italia68.github.io/Platemate in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button — the box with an arrow pointing up (↑) at the bottom of the screen
3
Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add in the top right — Platemate appears on your home screen like any app
🤖 Android — Chrome
1
Open italia68.github.io/Platemate in Chrome
2
Tap the ⋮ three-dot menu in the top right corner
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" — both do the same thing
4
Tap Add — Platemate installs to your home screen instantly
Using it on desktop? No install needed — just open the link in any browser and bookmark it. Chrome and Edge will also offer an "Install" option in the address bar for a desktop shortcut.
Getting Started

How to Log a Workout

Your first session takes about two minutes to set up. Here's what to do when you open the app.

1

Create a free account

Sign up with your email — no payment info, no App Store. Your workouts sync to the cloud so you never lose your history if you switch phones.

2

Tap + to add your first exercise

Hit the + button on the Today tab. Search for an exercise by name, or browse by muscle group. Can't find yours? Create a custom exercise in seconds.

3

Pick your equipment type

Choose Barbell (with plate visualizer), Dumbbell, Machine, or Other. The barbell view lets you tap plates on and off to calculate total weight — no math needed.

4

Log weight and reps per set

Enter reps for each individual set — not just a total. This is the key difference from most trackers. Your data stays honest: if set 3 dropped from 8 to 5 reps, that's recorded.

5

Check History and Stats

The History tab shows every past session by date. The Stats tab tracks your PRs automatically and shows a training heatmap so you can see consistency at a glance.

Quick Answers

The questions we get most often.

Does this work on iPhone?
Yes — open the link in Safari on any iPhone or iPad, then add it to your home screen using the Share button. It works just like a native app.
Does this work on Android?
Yes — open the link in Chrome on Android, tap the three-dot menu, and tap "Add to Home Screen." Done.
Is it actually free?
Yes. Completely free to use. No subscription, no payment info, no trial period.
Will my data be saved if I change phones?
Yes. Your workouts are saved to your account in the cloud — not just on your device. Sign in on any device and your full history is there.
Can I track cardio too?
Yes. Tap + on the Today tab and choose Cardio Session. Log duration, distance, and calories alongside your lifting — all in one place.
What if my exercise isn't in the list?
Create a custom exercise. In the exercise search screen, scroll to the bottom and tap "Create Custom Exercise." Name it, pick a muscle group, done.
Something isn't working — how do I get help?
Reach out via the contact page. We'll get back to you.
